Site Plan: EcoTrail Adventures

Site Name

EcoTrail Adventures

This name reflects the eco-friendly and nature-focused outdoor experiences the site will promote, such as hiking, nature tours, and guided trail explorations. It aligns with the brand’s mission to promote sustainable tourism and a love for nature.

Optional domain availability: ecotrailadventures.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for EcoTrail Adventures—a business offering eco-tourism hiking and trail packages. It will provide detailed information about available trail tours, booking options, sustainability practices, and a blog on nature topics. It also allows users to contact guides and join seasonal adventure events.

Scenarios

  • What types of hiking tours are available in the Pacific Northwest?
  • How can I book a private guided tour for my family?

Color Scheme

  • Forest Green (#3a6351): Used for headings, links, and buttons.
  • Soft White-Green (#f4f9f4): Used as the background color for the entire site.
  • Earth Brown (#2e3d2f): Used for main body text.

Typography

  • Roboto Slab (serif): Used for all headings to give a bold and grounded look.
  • Segoe UI (sans-serif): Used for body text for readability and modern style.
