Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cleaning services


How does the hiring process work?

Our process is simple: 1) Contact us by phone or web form, 2) We schedule a free evaluation, 3) You receive a personalized quote, 4) We schedule your first service. All in less than 24 hours.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire metropolitan area: San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, San Mateo and surrounding cities. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.

What's included in regular cleaning?

  • Surface cleaning and dusting
  • Vacuuming and mopping floors
  • Bathroom cleaning (toilets, showers, sinks)
  • Basic kitchen cleaning
  • Emptying trash bins

Do you do laundry or dishes?

Our standard service doesn't include laundry or dishes, but we can add it as an extra service for an additional cost.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations without charge. Cancellations with less than 12 hours notice may incur a 50% service fee.

Do you offer discounts for recurring services?

Yes, we offer 10% discount for weekly services and 5% for bi-weekly services. The discount applies after the first continuous month of service.

Are your employees insured?

Yes, all our employees are fully insured and have passed thorough background checks. We provide copies of our insurance upon request.

What COVID-19 protocols do you follow?

  • Mandatory mask usage
  • Daily temperature checks
  • Certified disinfectant products
  • Personal protective equipment
  • Social distancing when clients are present

Didn't find your answer?

We're here to help. Contact us directly and we'll answer all your questions.
